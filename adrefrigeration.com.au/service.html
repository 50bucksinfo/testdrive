Service

 
With our fully trained and equipped technicians at the ready the service department at A & D is the backbone of the company. We pride ourselves in providing precise and accurate system diagnosis and servicing, backed by our 24/7 emergency breakdown service to give our customers piece of mind and the comfort in knowing they will not be left stranded during an emergency. We also understand that our turnaround time affects the bottom line of our clients and we do our very best to manage each and every job to best serve the customer needs. We pride ourselves in offering many solutions to our customers which can help with energy management concerns to noise and vibration problems.
 
What we can service?
All commercial refrigeration & air conditioning, including;
  • Fridges, freezers and display units
  • Cool rooms and freezer rooms
  • Split and ducted air conditioning systems
  • Heating, ventilation and cooling systems
  • Chilled water systems
  • Large refrigeration and air conditioning plants
  • Production cooling and process cooling
  • Ice making machines
  • Humidification and dehumidification systems
