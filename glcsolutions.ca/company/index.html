A TEAM OF PASSIONATE INDIVIDUALS INSPIRED BY TECHNOLOGY AND LIFE

We help forward thinking companies do amazing things with technology. It all started in 2000, when our founder Glen Lieuwen opened up shop as an independent IT consultant to help small business owners implement technology. It wasn’t long before he realized the real value of what he was doing — providing business owners with strategic tools and actionable insights to help them succeed. And this business first approach is the beat of the drum we march to.

We’re one of the few IT service providers around that can analyze a business on a holistic level and understand the core needs that should drive key technology decisions and implement the right solution. From the first conversation to deployment and ongoing support, we’re there every step of the way. Have a problem that technology could solve? We’re your guys.

We focus on dialogue, collaboration and care about relationship building. That’s what makes us who we are. We genuinely care about people and their business, and it empowers us to deliver technology solutions that maximize value for every client, every time.
