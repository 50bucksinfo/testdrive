Group exercise classes are designed to be motivating and fun. They help you to improve your cardiovascular fitness, strength and flexibility, and with over 34 classes weekly we are sure to have a class that you'll love.
 

All of our instructors are fully qualified and pride themselves on being some of Sydneys' best instructors.

Our group exercise room is fully airconditioned, and underlayed, to reduce impact and stress on your body.

With its high timber ceilings, our group exercise room has a great atmosphere for classes such as yoga. A lot of ladies also like the group exercise room as it allows a level of privacy. 

 

ZUMBA
A high energy dance style class with a Latin flavour where you can really shake your booty, have fun and get fit at the same time.

 

BODYFIRM

A non-impact strength class using barbells and weights. All the major muscle groups are covered in this great all over toning class. You are in control of how much weight is on your barbell which means you are in control of your workout! The instructor focuses on light weights and high repetition to tone and strengthen muscles. 

 

 

TUMS, BUMS AND THIGHS

A high intensity, boot-camp style class with focus on all those problem areas that we all struggle to tone. You will get an intensive work out with this class! Be prepared to crunch, squat and lunge - It's all in the title of the class!

 

 

CYCLE

This class is the fastest class to burn calories and increase cardiovascular fitness. The instructor takes the class on a journey through different terrains and styles; up hill, flat ground, sprints and jumps. You are in control of your own bikes resistance level so if you are just starting out you can take it easy or, if you are a regular exerciser, you can make it as hard as you like! Great for the beginner or the advanced participant. Make sure you book for this class to reserve your bike.

 

 

PILATES

A class that aims to develop the body's "centre" in order to create a stable core for all types of movement. This means there are a lot of abdominal and back exercises designed to increase strength, flexibility, posture, & reduce the risk of back injury. Most PILATES exercises are stationery and performed whilst laying on the floor. There is no choreography and NO IMPACT on joints, making it ideal for everyone. Each exercise has different levels from, easy to difficult, resulting in this class being great for the beginner or the advanced participant.

 

 

YOGA

Designed to tone, strengthen, and align the body. Yoga classes increase flexibility, suppleness, inner strength and can aid in stress management. Yoga postures are performed to make the spine supple and healthy, and to promote blood flow to all the bodies organs, keeping all the bodily systems in balance. On the mental level, yoga uses breathing techniques and meditation to quiet, clarify, and discipline the mind. 

 

HATHA YOGA - A discipline that helps unify mind and body. There is a natural realignment of the body when postures are held and synchronised with breath.

 

POWER VINYASA YOGA - A dynamic flowing style of yoga that will give you a real work out.

 

JAPANESE YOGA - Based on the 5 Element theory (the principals of Yin & Yang) Japanese yoga uses repetitive movement, traditional Hatha postures and breath to give the individual a greater sense of balance, self awareness, strength and wellness.

 

YIN YOGA - Enjoy a slower Yin Yoga class in the evening to allow your body to deeply open and unwind from the day. A yin practice calms your nervous system and internally cleanses the organs while deeply explores hip openers and strengthening the core in longer holds using deeper breaths. 

 

 

LATIN CARDIO
Combines a cardio workout with technique and posture tips to improve your Latin dancing style. The class is a continuous mix of salsa, samba, meringue, cha cha, rumba, and mambo and jive styles, combined into just the right blend of sweat, technique improvement and fun!

 

BALLET STRETCH

 

combines stretching with elements of ballet (e.g plies, tendues, port des bras) to increase flexibility posture, balance, coordination and wellbeing.
