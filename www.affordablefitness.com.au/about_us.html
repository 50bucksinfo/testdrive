Who does Affordable Fitness cater for?



We pride ourselves on being a friendly gym and we are a strong part of the Balmain community.

We basically cater for everyone!
 
We especially like to look after our mums and our seniors, and helping boys and girls from as young as 13.
 
For the guys, we have all the equipment you need to get in shape and have a great muscular physique.
 
Join Affordable Fitness and you will get to know that our members are just as friendly as we are. You'll meet local professionals, artists, mums, dads, academics, and tradespeople. People who are looking for a friendly atmosphere where they know they are valued.
 
We all know each other by name and we look forward to meeting you.

Our aim is to provide our members with exceptional service and a facility that will enable them to achieve and maintain their fitness and lifestyle goals.

Affordable Fitness is fully air-conditioned, with state of the art equipment, and a very comfortable atmosphere. A lot of our members like us because we are not a typical intimidating gym environment.
