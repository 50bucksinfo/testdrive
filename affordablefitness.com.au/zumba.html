Zumba is our exciting new class. Zumba is a high energy dance style class with a Latin flavour  where you can really shake your booty, have fun, and get fit at the same time. We have Zumba classes almost every day of the week at different times in the morning and evening to try and suit everyone.

Don't worry if you're not a great dancer, or don't have much coordination. Everyone in this class is just having a party and at the end you'll walk out all hot and sweaty. We promise!
