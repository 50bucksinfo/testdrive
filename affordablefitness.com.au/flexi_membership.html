 At Affordable Fitness, we understand that a full membership isn't for everyone. What if you only want to come once a week for that certain class you love, or only have one free day that you can possibly make it to the gym? Introducing the new...
 
 
$12 PER WEEK
 
FLEXI MEMBERSHIP
 
 
This membership simply allows you to come once per week. Use the gym, attend a class, whatever you do, it's up to you! Your $12p/w, debited out of your bank account, allows you complete access to all gym facilities.
 
But what happens if you get busy and miss coming one week? Easy! Simply make it up by coming twice the following week.
 
A nice  and simple membership. The way it should be!
